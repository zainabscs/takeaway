Our Story

Born from the Flame

SMOKD started with a single grill, a family recipe, and an obsession with getting it right. Every patty is hand-pressed, every bowl is assembled fresh, and every bite carries the char of live fire.

We believe great food doesn't need shortcuts. Our beef is locally sourced, our chicken is free-range, and our sauces are made in-house. From the moment you order to the last bite — everything is intentional.
